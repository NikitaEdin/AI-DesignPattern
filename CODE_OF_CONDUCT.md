# Code of Conduct

## Academic Integrity
This research project adheres to the highest standards of academic integrity and ethical research practices.

## Research Standards
This project maintains:
* Strict methodology and transparent documentation.
* Ethical use of LLM APIs and generated content.
* Responsible data handling and privacy protection.

## Academic Ethics
All aspects of this research follow:
* University research ethics guidelines.
* Academic honesty principles and supervision.
* Responsible AI research practices.
* Open science and reproducibility standards.

## Purpose
This Code of Conduct ensures the research maintains academic credibility and contributes positively to the scientific community's understanding of LLM capabilities in software engineering.
