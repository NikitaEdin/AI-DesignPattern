# Contributing
This is an academic research project for a Honours thesis.

> [!CAUTION]
> Pull requests and direct commits are paused until the end of the project deadline to maintain academic integrity and research independence during the Honours submission period.

## Purpose
This repository serves as:
- Documentation of research methodology and implementation.
- Code archive for reproducibility and academic review.
- Reference material for future research in this domain.

## Academic Use
If you use this research or codebase in your academic work:
- Please provide proper citation.
- Acknowledge the original research context.
- Follow academic integrity guidelines.

## Research Context
This project represents original Honours research investigating LLM efficiency in design pattern recognition.<br>
The codebase and methodology are made available for transparency and reproducibility in academic research.
